# Translating

You can help to translate amen Share X into your language.
On [Crowdin](https://crowdin.com/project/amen-share-x) you can easily translate amen Share X online.

Is your language not on Crowdin? Feel free to [Request it](https://github.com/amen-edu/amen-share-x/issues/new?assignees=&labels=language-request&projects=&template=language-request.yml&title=%F0%9F%8C%90+Language+request%3A+%3Clanguage+name+in+english%3E).
